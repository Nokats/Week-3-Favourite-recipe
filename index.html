Mihkel's legendary famous pancake recipe

Ingredient Amount
Eggs 5 large
Sugar 50g
Salt 0,5tsp
Milk 500ml
Water 100ml
Flour 250g
  1. Whisk eggs
  2. Add sugar, salt and whisk
  3. Add milk and whisk
  4. Add water and whisk
  5. Add flour and whisk
  6. Fry the pancakes
  7. Enjoy!
